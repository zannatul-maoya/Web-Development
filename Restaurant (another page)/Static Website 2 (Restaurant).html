Apple Cake with Caramel Sauce

Delicious obsessions

11

|

55

|

380

Ingredient

Mineral

Calories

Ingredient

210 gram plain flour
1 large red apple
2 cup suger
2 large egg
1/2 tablespoon melted butter
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
120 ml vegetable oil
120 ml yoogurt
2 teaspoons baking powder
11/2 teaspoon baking soda
2 tablespoons milk
30 gm power suger

Method

1 Beat butter and suger
2 Beat the two eggs untill smooth and creamy
3 After beating mix them properly in a mixing bowl
4 Add vanilla extract in the mixing bowl
5 Sift flour,baking soda and baking powder together in another bowl
6 Then mix all of them and pour cake pans
7 Now make caramel and add apple in this pan
8 Gently pour cake pans with apple caramel mixture in the top
9 Bake it about 25 to 35 min.
